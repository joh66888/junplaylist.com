ABOUT

jun.playlist®—K-Pop Music Poster Series is a self-initiated project designed by Jun. It has been a continuous work in progress, leading up to the development of this website in collaboration with Ji this year. This interactive website showcases diverse typographic posters inspired by K-pop songs. The website offers a unique real-time listening experience, immersing users in the captivating world of K-pop. Users can embark on a journey of discovery, appreciation, and celebration of the vibrant K-pop culture through this platform.
